How is Launch Code helping aspiring programmers?

Launchcode is a non- for profit organization who saw the need for computer programmers at companies in st.louis, but the lack of available candidates sparked the swift action launchcode to help the cause. They went to a 100 HR departments at various companies and asked if they could get them the talent, would they try the candidates out. All the companies agreed and that was the start of what has grown today into an inniative that President Obama endorsed. They offer classes to people with no programming experience looking to get into the field. They pair you up with companies your skills are suitable for, and your interview for a paid intership. It's the best thing since color tv. With all the resources they provide, there are no barriers to entry.
